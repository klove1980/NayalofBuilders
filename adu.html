Why Build an ADU?

💰

Rental Income

Generate $1,100–$1,500+ per month in rental income. With Maryland's proximity to D.C. and Baltimore, demand for rental units remains strong.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Multigenerational Living

Keep aging parents close while maintaining privacy and independence. An ADU provides a perfect solution for multigenerational families.

🏖️

Vacation Rental

List your ADU on Airbnb or VRBO. Maryland's location near tourist destinations makes it ideal for short-term rentals.

📈

Property Value

ADUs can increase your property value by 20-30%. It's a smart investment that pays dividends when you decide to sell.

🏠

Home Office

Create a separate, professional workspace without distractions. Perfect for remote workers, consultants, or small business owners.

🎓

Adult Children

Provide a space for adult children transitioning to independence, whether they're starting careers or saving for their own home.
